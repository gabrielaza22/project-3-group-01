    Biodiversity in National Parks

    This website explores biodiversity in the National Parks in America, using data from the National Park Service species survey in 2016. These analyses focuses on the number of endangered species in each park. Note that the map page shows the location of each park in the country and the relative size of each. The circle marker sizes are not indicative of actual park size in relation to the map..
